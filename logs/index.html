LOG #001
13th of Humbertum, 1777
Cidade de Bergia, Central Prefecture, Bergia

My wings are slightly damaged from yesterday's flight with Eliza, she's been caring for me, I love her a lot. I have been instructed to start a diary by Professoressa Escolastica. I do not really understand why she decided I should write a diary (maybe the fact I'll probably outlive her?) but she asked, so I'm going to do it.

Emilia Clio


LOG #002
14th of Humbertum, 1777
Cidade de Bergia, Central Prefecture, Bergia

Wings have recovered, the cut was minor anyways. I don't have much to talk about today. Recent news coming out of another corruption scandal, involves a bank this time, don't know much about it, don't care too, these things start to blur together after five hundred years. Eliza told me this is possibly the biggest one yet, I trust her, you don't marry someone you don't trust, and she's seen a lot too, so maybe I should follow it closer, I don't know.

Apparently the Congregation's leader has died, they're having one of those elections they do when their leader dies. I'm not religious, but it's interesting to read about it, they've been around this world for longer than me. For my personal life, not much has happened, just kept writing the Manuscrito, trying to finish the Bergia entry so I can move on to other nations.

Emilia Clio
