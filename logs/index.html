From Emilia Clio's Personal Diary

LOG #001
13th of Humbertum, 1777

My wings are slightly damaged from yesterday's flight with Eliza. I have been instructed to start a diary by Professoressa Escolastica. I do not really understand why she decided I should write a diary (maybe the fact I'll probably outlive her?) but she asked, so I'm going to do it.
