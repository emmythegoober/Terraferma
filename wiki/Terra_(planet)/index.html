Terra (planet)

From Emilia Clio's Manuscrito Global

Terra (tehr-RAH), also known as PEO3 (Post-Esquecida Object III), is a hypothetical interstellar planet that collided with primordial Terraferma after being violently ejected from its system. It is one of the possible explanations for the formation of Lua Major. Terra is hypothesized to have travelled from a one-star system after the explosion of its sun. According to some conspiracy theorists, it is the place of origin for the Disco Dourado, but most scientist believe the disc is from Terraferma.
